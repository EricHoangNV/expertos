# John&Partners - Operational Excellence Podcast

- **Host:** TS. Ngô Công Trường (DBA, PhD Economics, European University)
- **Total Episodes:** 168 (as of 04/06/2023)
- **Key Themes:**
  - **Operational Excellence (OPEX):** Focus on optimizing business operations.
  - **Lean Six Sigma:** Methodology for process improvement and waste reduction.
  - **OKR/KPI/SMART:** Tools for measuring business objectives.
  - **Hoshin Kanri, OGSM, BSC:** Strategic planning frameworks.
  - **IPO & Fundraising:** Insights on initial public offerings and capital raising.
  - **COVID Management:** Strategies for business continuity during the pandemic.
  - **Digital Transformation:** Preparing businesses for digital changes.
  - **World-Class Manufacturing:** Principles for achieving high standards in manufacturing.

- **Distinctive Viewpoint:**
  - Emphasizes the importance of continuous learning and adaptation in business management.
  - Advocates for a structured approach to operational excellence through established frameworks and methodologies.

- **Frameworks and Methods:**
  - **Lean Six Sigma:** Combines Lean manufacturing and Six Sigma to improve performance by systematically removing waste.
  - **Hoshin Kanri:** A strategic planning process that aligns an organization’s functions and activities with its strategic goals.
  - **OGSM (Objectives, Goals, Strategies, Measures):** A framework for setting and tracking business objectives.
  - **BSC (Balanced Scorecard):** A performance management tool that provides a view of an organization from multiple perspectives.

- **Key Recommendations:**
  - Businesses should adopt a culture of reskilling and upskilling to remain competitive.
  - Implementing effective crisis management strategies is crucial for survival during challenging times.
  - Companies should prepare thoroughly before undergoing digital transformation to ensure successful implementation.

- **Notable Episodes:**
  - **Episode 001:** RESKILL & UPSKILL for the new era.
  - **Episode 084:** Lean Six Sigma tools in Operational Excellence.
  - **Episode 155-156:** World-Class Manufacturing principles.

- **Future Trends:**
  - Focus on ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) criteria as a growing concern for businesses.
  - Anticipation of changes in HR and management practices by 2030.
