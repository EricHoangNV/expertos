# Limitless Success - Dr. Ngo Cong Truong

- **Core Claims:**
  - Success has no age limit, starting point, industry, or hierarchy.
  - Anyone can achieve success regardless of their background or current position.
  - The U.S. market offers vast opportunities for business growth, especially for franchises.

- **Frameworks/Models:**
  - **Franchise Model:** A smart leverage strategy to expand a business using the strengths of others.
    - Example: Remax in real estate, which was designed for franchising from inception.
  
- **Key Numbers/Criteria:**
  - Over 1,000 clients consulted in 15 years.
  - Established 28 companies, with John and Partners being the first.
  - Achieved recognition as one of the top 40 experts globally in 2018.

- **Distinctive Points of View:**
  - The U.S. is a more favorable environment for business compared to Vietnam, likening it to a "ocean" of opportunities versus a "pond."
  - Emphasizes the importance of preparation and resilience in overcoming challenges in business.
  - Advocates for a mindset that embraces challenges and views them as opportunities for growth.
