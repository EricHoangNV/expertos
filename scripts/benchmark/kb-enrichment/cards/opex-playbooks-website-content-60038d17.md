### Playbook Structure (8-Part Format)
1. **Problem Statement:** Define the business challenge.
2. **Framework & Core Concepts:** Theoretical foundation.
3. **Step-by-Step Guide:** Practical implementation steps.
4. **Templates & Checklists:** Ready-to-use tools.
5. **Exercises:** Practice activities.
6. **Roadmap:** Implementation timeline.
7. **Action Steps Summary:** Quick reference guide.
8. **Conclusion + Call to Action:** Next steps.
