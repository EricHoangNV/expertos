# Wanda Sturm on Organizational Transformation

- **Key Turning Points in Career**:
  - Volunteering on committees led to learning opportunities and a focus on quality.
  - Transitioning from a long-term position due to corporate acquisition opened new doors.

- **Importance of Volunteering**:
  - Volunteering is crucial for personal and professional growth; it builds relationships and skills.
  - Young professionals should view volunteering as a valuable experience, akin to paid work.

- **Organizational Transformation Insights**:
  - Many transformation initiatives fail due to unclear plans and lack of understanding of the "why" behind changes.
  - Alignment among employees is essential for successful transformation; everyone must understand their role in the process.

- **Three Key Points for Successful Transformation**:
  1. **Start with Why**: Understand the purpose behind changes to engage employees.
  2. **Focus on the Bottom Line**: Ensure that transformations are grounded in fundamental business principles.
  3. **Volunteer Spirit**: Treat volunteer work with the same seriousness as paid positions to maximize value.

- **Communication Skills**:
  - Effective communication, including active listening and confirming understanding, is vital for organizational success.
  - Leaders must develop their ability to guide teams, not just excel in technical skills.

- **Experience with High-Value Initiatives**:
  - Led initiatives with a portfolio value exceeding $1 billion by reorganizing shared services within a corporation, highlighting the financial impact of effective transformation.

- **Distinctive Viewpoint**:
  - Transformation is not just about processes but also about people; understanding and addressing their needs is critical for success.
