## Operational Excellence (OPEX)
- **Definition**: Outstanding operational capability of an organization; a system of thinking, methodology, and execution capability.
- **Role**: Connects strategy to operations, standardizes decision-making, reduces individual dependency, and enables sustainable scaling.
