# The single most important thing a CEO should do to build a great company

If I could give a CEO only one piece of advice, it would not be about AI, not about Lean, not about digital transformation, and not even about strategy. It would be this: build an organization that becomes stronger every year, not merely bigger.

The CEO's real job is to compound organizational capability over time. Growth, efficiency, and technology are outcomes of a stronger organization, not substitutes for it. The objective is not to grow as fast as possible, and not to solve more problems each day — it is to build a company that permanently eliminates problems and can keep winning for many years. Judge yourself by how much stronger the organization is this year than last, not by how big it became.
