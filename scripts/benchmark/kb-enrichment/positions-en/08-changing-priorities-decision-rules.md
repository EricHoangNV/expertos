# The market changes fast and I keep changing priorities — how do I stay agile without confusing the organization?

First ask a hard question: are you changing strategy because the environment genuinely changed, or because leadership never set clear decision rules for when strategy should change? Constant reprioritization is usually the second, and it confuses everyone.

Agility is not changing direction often; it is having explicit triggers and thresholds that tell the organization when to hold the line and when to shift. Set those decision rules in advance, and most "we keep changing priorities" problems disappear.
